For better or worse, the general public has been more interested in Marc Daly since first meeting him on The Real Housewives of Atlanta through his now-wife, Kenya Moore. This seems to be mostly for the worse as the couple is now breaking up. Though Moore is the television personality and former beauty queen, Daly has reportedly amassed a huge network due to his career and business.

Who is Marc Daly?

Daly, primarily based in New York City, is a businessman and restaurant owner. In June 2017, he and Moore got married. Moore announced they were pregnant with their first child the following year.

Their daughter, Brooklyn Doris Daly, was born on November 4, 2018. The couple named her Brooklyn because Daly and Moore first met in the New York City borough of Brooklyn.

Marc Daly and Kenya Moore have had a tumultuous relationship

The couple’s relationship has been troubled since pretty much the star. Moore and Daly broke up in 2018. At this time, Moore said she was filing for divorce. Despite this, they appared to continue to stay together and co-parented their daughter.

Moore told US Weekly in November 2019, “I do think that things could get back on track if you have two people that want to. It’s good. We’re very cordial, very kind to each other right now. We’re working really well together and just being there for our daughter. I can’t really hope for anything else right now in terms of how we’re co-parenting. It’s been really wonderful so far.”

Though they tried, it looks like the couple is now done for good. In an statement last week, Daly confirmed to The Root that they were breaking up. “After recently attending mediation, Kenya and I have agreed to end our marriage,” he said in a statement to the publication. “I will always have great care for her and look forward to amicably continuing to co-parent our daughter Brooklyn with an abundance of love and wisdom.”

Marc Daly is known for his restaraunt

Not much is known about Daly’s personal life outside of his marriage with Moore that played out on The Real Housewives of Atlanta. However, we do know a good amount of information about his professional life.

He was in the banking industry before becoming a restauranteur. He owns the Southern fusion restaurant, SoCo, which is a popular restaurant that is based in Brooklyn. According to JustRichest, his net worth is estimated to be around $35 million.

Because of Moore’s profile, their split will definitely continue to play out in the public eye.
