Bronx Twins Dead, 5 Year Old Twins Found Dead In A Apartment In New York

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Bronx Twins Dead: In a heartbreaking incident, a 5-year-old twin boy and girl were discovered lifeless inside their New York City apartment on Monday. The grim discovery was made by their mother, who alerted authorities through a 911 call at 11:20 a.m. local time. The tragic event unfolded at 240 East 175th Street in the Bronx.

Upon arrival, the NYPD found the young siblings “unconscious and unresponsive” as detailed in a statement obtained by PEOPLE. Emergency Medical Services (EMS) promptly responded, pronouncing both children dead at the scene. The nature of their demise remains shrouded in mystery, and an ongoing investigation is underway. The Office of the Chief Medical Examiner will play a pivotal role in determining the cause of their untimely deaths.

Disturbing Details Emerge

Disturbing details have emerged regarding the hours leading up to the tragic discovery. According to the NYPD spokesperson, the twins’ mother found them in a dire state inside the apartment. No specific details were provided on the circumstances that preceded the 911 call, leaving the public in suspense.

In light of the distressing incident, CBS New York reported that there were no apparent signs of trauma on the children’s bodies. Moreover, investigators found no traces of carbon monoxide, a common silent killer, in the residence. Officials also confirmed that the family did not have a history of domestic violence, further deepening the mystery surrounding the tragic event.
