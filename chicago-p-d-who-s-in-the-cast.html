Chicago P.D.: Whos in the Cast?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

If you’re starting to watch Chicago P.D., you might want to know which actors star in the show. Here’s a rundown of the main Chicago P.D. cast members.

Jason Beghe

Jason Beghe plays Hank Voight. Beghe made his acting debut in the 1985 film Compromising Positions. The following year, he appeared in the TV mini-series Dress Gray. That year, he also joined the cast of 1st and Ten. He played the role of Tom Yinessa for 23 episodes from 1986 to 1987.

Beghe appeared in his second movie, Made to Order, in 1987. He played the role of Bret. Beghe is also known for his roles in Chicago Hope, Melrose Place, Californication, and American Dreams.

Jesse Lee Soffer

Jesse Lee Soffer plays Jay Halstead. One of Soffer’s early acting roles was in the 1993 movie Matinee. The following year, he appeared in the movie Safe Passage. Soffer then played the role of Bobby Brady in The Brady Bunch Movie.

In 1998, Soffer landed a recurring role in the ABC television series Two of a Kind, starring Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen. He played the character Taylor Donovan for six episodes.

Soffer rose to fame after starring in the daytime drama As the World Turns. He played Will Munson from 2004 to 2010. Soffer is also known for his roles in The Mentalist, The Mob Doctor, and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

Tracy Spiridakos

Tracy Spiridakos plays Hailey Upton. Spiridakos made her television acting debut in a 2007 episode of Supernatural titled “Bedtime Stories.” That same year, she played Liz in an episode of Aliens in America titled “Church.”

Spiridakos made her film debut in the 2010 movie The Boy She Met Online. She’s also known for her appearances in Mortal Kombat: Legacy, Being Human, Revolution, and Majority Rules!

Marina Squerciati

Marina Squerciati plays Kim Burgess. Squerciati made her acting debut in the 1993 movie The Nutcracker. She made her television acting debut in a 2009 episode of Law & Order: Criminal Intent titled “Revolution.” She played the role of Betsy Naylor.

Squerciati rose to fame after appearing in the series Gossip Girl. She played the character Alessandra Steele for eight episodes. Squerciati is also known for her roles in Special Skills, Central Park, and Marshall.

LaRoyce Hawkins

LaRoyce Hawkins plays Kevin Atwater. Hawkins made his television acting debut in a 2008 episode of House of Payne. That same year, he appeared in the movie The Express, in which he played the role of Art Baker. Hawkins’ first recurring role was in the television series South Side.

In 2019, Hawkins played Michael “Shaw” Owens for five episodes. Hawkins is also known for his roles in Detroit 1-8-7, Chicago Justice, Canal Street, and The Killing of Kenneth Chamberlain.

Patrick Flueger

Patrick Flueger plays Adam Ruzek. Flueger made his acting debut in the 2001 movie The Princess Diaries. The following year, he appeared in an episode of Septuplets as the character Zeke Wilde.

Flueger rose to fame after joining the cast of The 4400. He played Shawn Farrell from 2004 to 2007. Flueger is also known for his roles in Scoundrels, Brothers, and Kill Theory.

Amy Morton

Amy Morton plays Trudy Platt. Morton made her acting debut in the 1983 TV movie Through Naked Eyes. She played the character Karen. From 1986 to 1987, Morton had a recurring role in the series Crime Story. Morton made her big-screen debut in the 1992 movie Straight Talk. She is also known for her appearances in Blue Bloods, Homeland, 8MM,and Boss.

Chicago P.D. airs Wednesdays on NBC at 10:00 p.m. Eastern Time.
