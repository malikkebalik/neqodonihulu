John Deere 5500 Utility Tractor is equipped with a John Deere turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 239 ci [3.9 L] engine, which delivers 83.2 hp [62.0 kW] power at rated rpm 2400.

John Deere 5500 Utility Tractor featured with a John Deere 3.9L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, 3,374 lbs [1530 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 5,780 lbs [2621 kg] weight, 9 qts [8.5 L] engine oil capacity, and 9 forward and 3 reverse gears.

John Deere, 5500 Utility tractor attachments include John Deere 520 and John Deere 540 MSL front-end loader.
