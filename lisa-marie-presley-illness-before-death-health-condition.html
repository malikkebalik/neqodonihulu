Lisa Marie Presley Illness Before Death: Last Health Condition

Lisa Marie Presley Personal Life

Lisa was born on February 1, 1968, in midtown Memphis, Tennessee, to Elvis and Priscilla Presley. 

She moved to Los Angeles at 4 with her mother after her parents’ 1973 divorce. On October 3, 1988, Presley married musician Danny Keough.

The couple welcomed two children; a daughter, Riley Keough, an actress and model, and a son, Benjamin Storm Keough.

But, on May 6, 1994, she divorced her husband in the Dominican Republic.

Marie wedded singer Michael Jackson 20 days after her divorce. When she was seven, they met while attending several of his Las Vegas concerts.

However, she filed for divorce in January 1996, alleging irreconcilable differences. In 2000, she became engaged to musician John Oszajca.

After meeting Nicolas Cage at a party, Presley called off the wedding and married Cage on August 10, 2002; they divorced in 2004.

Her fourth and last marriage was with Michael Lockwood, a musician, music producer, and director, on January 22, 2006.

The late singer gave birth to fraternal twin girls named Harper Vivienne Ann Lockwood and Finley Aaron Love Lockwood on October 7, 2008.

After ten years of marriage, Lisa filed for divorce from Lockwood in 2016.
