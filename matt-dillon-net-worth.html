Matt Dillon net worth:

$35 million

Matt Dillon is an American actor who made his acting debut in the late 1970s with the movie Over the Edge. In the 1980s, he was able to establish himself as a teen idol with the successes of the movies My Bodyguard, Tex, Rumble Fish, and The Outsiders. He continued his success with the release of the feature films Drugstore Cowboy, Singles, Beautiful Girls, and There’s Something About Mary. Staying active on the big screen, he’s had roles in Crash, You, Me and Dupree. He was also nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his performance in Crash. His recent work was on the TV series Wayward Pines.
