Why does my turkey still look pink?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Nitrites and nitrates are often added to cured meats, like bacon and ham, to make them pink. But they also occur naturally in vegetables and water. So, turkeys are exposed to both through their food. If enough nitrites and nitrates are present, it can give the meat a pink tinge.Click to see full answer.
