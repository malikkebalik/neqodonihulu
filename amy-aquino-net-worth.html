Amy Aquino Net Worth

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Amy Aquino

$65 million

Amy Aquino is an American actress whose career spans decades. She debuted in the 1987 film Moonstruck, followed by a role in the movie Working Girl. In the 1990s, she had performances in the films Descending Angel, Alan & Naomi, and Boys on the Side. On TV, her first starring role was as Phyllis Berger Silver on Brooklyn Bridge. She later starred as Sasha Danziger on Madman of the People and as Dr. Joanna “Joey” Diamond on Picket Fences. Aquino then played Dr. Janet Coburn on ER. In the 2000s, she played Dr. Toni Pavone on Felicity, Detective Lois Carveron Crossing Jordan, and Peggy Ardolino on Everybody Loves Raymond. During this period, she had roles in the films White Oleander, In Good Company, A Lot Like Love, and In My Sleep. As of 2014, she stars as Lieutenant Grace Billets on Amazon’s Bosch.
